Process of creating

  • 01. Objective
  • 02. Design
  • 03. Result

My objective in designing a site is to ensure that when the users open up the site they see the information in a format that is easy to read, accessible, and relevant.


To ensure that the site will come up correctly in different browsers (cross-browser), different operating systems (crossplatform) and different devices (cross-device).


  • Your site will weigh and run just as much as the search engine rank a site.
  • Your site will become cheap to host and secured because it doesn't include any dynamic content that can be generated by server code.
  • Your site will use modern web technologies(html5, css3, jQuery, and javascript).
  • Your site will be in "Fluid-12-column-responsive system" that scales and adjust according to the size of the device. (Mobile first approach).
  • Your site will only need off-page SEO.

Without your users, You have nothing
